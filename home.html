Interesting Facts About Camel

1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
3. Camels have two rows of thick eyelashes to protect their eyes from the desert dust.

Interesting Facts About Dog

1. Dogs can sniff at the same time as breathing.
2. They don't sweat like we do.
3. Their sense of smell is at least 40x better than ours.

Interesting Facts About Cat

1. Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
2. The hearing of the average cat is at least five times keener than that of a human adult.
3. Cats are believed to be the only mammals who don't taste sweetness.
